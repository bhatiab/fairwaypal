# Mugello First-Time Attendee — Fact Check

Researched: April 2026
Source: Web research via MotoGPMugello.com, SitWhere.com, TripAdvisor, Tuscany.tips, News.GP

---

## Circuit facts

- Circuit length: 5.245 km, 15 corners
- Location: Scarperia, Tuscany, ~30 km northeast of Florence
- All-time MotoGP top speed record: 366+ km/h (set on the main straight)
- First corner San Donato: braking from 350+ km/h to ~92 km/h
- Sunday attendance: regularly exceeds 90,000
- Venue is owned by Ferrari

## Grandstand names (verified from official circuit map)

- Centrale (Bronze/Silver/Poltronissima) — Main straight / pit lane / podium
- Grandstand 58 / Arrabbiata area — Turns 11-12 (Arrabbiata 2 / Bucine section)
- Poggio Secco — Turn 3, sightlines across Turns 2–7
- Materassi — adjacent to Poggio Secco
- General Admission hillside — Curvone (Turn 8) and surrounding hills

## Circuit entrances

- **Palagio entrance**: Poltronissima, Silver, Biondetti, Bronze grandstands
- **Luco entrance**: Poggio Secco, Materassi, Grandstand 58 / Arrabbiata area
- CRITICAL: getting this wrong means a 30-45 minute walk between entrances

## Transport (verified)

- Train: Florence SMN → Borgo San Lorenzo (~50-70 min, Trenitalia regional) OR San Piero a Sieve
- Shuttle buses from Borgo San Lorenzo to circuit gates (race weekend only, typically 07:00–18:00)
- BusForFun: official partner, direct Florence-to-circuit coaches; busforfun.com
- Bus Line 307A (Autolinee Toscane) from Florence to Borgo San Lorenzo — same connection needed
- Driving: A1 autostrada → Barberino di Mugello exit → ~40 min from Florence in normal traffic
- Sunday morning: leave accommodation before 07:00 to avoid traffic
- Race start: typically 14:00 local

## Food near the circuit

- Tortelli di patate (potato-filled pasta): the Mugello valley signature dish
- Pappardelle al cinghiale (wide pasta with wild boar ragu): authentic regional dish
- Bistecca alla Fiorentina: thick T-bone from Chianina cattle, Florence/Scarperia
- Scarperia options: Ristorante degli Artisti, Quindicicurve Pizzeria, Antica Osteria di Nandone
- San Piero a Sieve: Osteria di San Piero
- Circuit food: better than most circuits; panini, BBQ, croissants ~€6; bring water

## Weather (late May / early June)

- Daytime highs: 22-28°C on clear days
- June is tail end of spring wet season — afternoon thunderstorms possible
- Circuit is at ~300m elevation, slightly cooler than Florence
- Hillside GA areas have zero shade from late morning
