## Methodology

Ratings (1–5 scale) are based on fan experience factors, not race on-track action.
Sources used: official circuit websites, MotoGP fan forums, travel review sites, and cost data from Sprint 2.

---

## Per-circuit review

### Jerez
- **Accessibility (4):** Shuttle from Jerez city is €1.10. Seville airport (SVQ) ~1h15m by car. Good connections. ✓
- **Atmosphere (5):** One of the most attended GPs in the world. 100k+ on race Sunday. ✓
- **Value for money (4):** GA from €69 early bird; camping available. One of the cheaper southern European rounds. ✓
- **Beginner friendly (5):** Clear shuttle, easy logistics, English widely spoken. ✓
- **Weather reliability (4):** Late April averages 23°C. Spring showers possible but rare. ✓
- **Weather description:** Spot-check needed — verify April averages vs May dates if calendar shifts.

### Le Mans
- **Accessibility (4):** TGV from Paris (55 min). Good flight connections to Paris CDG. ✓
- **Value for money (5):** Camping included with ticket is unique on the calendar. Under-16s free. ✓
- **Weather reliability (2):** May at Le Mans is historically variable — accurate. ✓
- **VERIFY:** Confirm "under-16s free for GA" is still policy for 2026. Was true in 2025.

### Catalunya
- **Accessibility (5):** R2 commuter train from Barcelona central to Montmeló (25 min). Excellent airport. ✓
- **Value for money (3):** Barcelona is expensive for accommodation — accurate. ✓
- **Weather reliability (4):** June Mediterranean = very reliable. ✓

### Mugello
- **Accessibility (3):** No direct public transport to circuit. Need car or shuttle from Scarperia. Limited rail. ✓
- **Value for money (2):** GA from €135 (cheapest option in Sprint 2 data). One of the priciest GAs on calendar. ✓
- **Atmosphere (5):** Italian tifosi — widely regarded as among the best atmospheres. ✓
- **VERIFY:** Mugello Verde campsite pricing — confirm still operational and bookable.

### Hungary (Balaton Park)
- **All ratings:** This is a brand-new circuit for MotoGP 2026. Ratings are estimates based on F1 Hungarian GP experience and circuit location.
- **HUMAN REVIEW REQUIRED:** Accessibility, atmosphere, beginner-friendliness ratings should be re-evaluated after the first race.
- **Note:** 1.5-hour drive from Budapest is a real limitation for public transport access. Rating of 3 for accessibility is appropriate.

### Brno
- **Value for money (5):** Beer, food, accommodation all cheaper than Western Europe. Wave 1 tickets significantly cheaper. ✓
- **Accessibility (3):** No direct flights from many UK airports. Need Vienna (VIE) or driving. ✓
- **VERIFY:** Confirm Brno (BRQ) airport operational status — had limited scheduled routes.

### Assen
- **Atmosphere (5):** "The Cathedral" — universally regarded as one of MotoGP's best atmospheres. ✓
- **Weather reliability (2):** Dutch late June weather is notoriously unpredictable. ✓
- **Value for money (3):** Tickets from €189 for GA embankments. Hotels sell out months ahead and price up significantly. ✓
- **VERIFY:** Confirm GA embankment ticket pricing for 2026 — only 2 tiers in Sprint 2 data (no VIP listed).

### Sachsenring
- **Accessibility (3):** Leipzig/Halle (LEJ) or Dresden (DRS) airports. No direct international connections for most. ✓
- **Atmosphere (4):** Good but smaller than Assen/Mugello. German crowd is enthusiastic but circuit is compact. ✓
- **VERIFY:** ADAC 10% discount still available for 2026 season?

### Silverstone
- **Accessibility (4):** Train to Milton Keynes + race shuttle. No flights needed for UK fans. ✓
- **Weather reliability (2):** British summer weather — accurate. ✓
- **Value for money (3):** UK prices are high. GA from ~£178. ✓

### Aragon
- **Value for money (5):** GA from €79 — one of cheapest on calendar. ✓
- **Accessibility (3):** Alcañiz is remote. Fly into Zaragoza or Barcelona then drive. ✓
- **Weather reliability (4):** September Aragonese weather is very reliable and dry. ✓

### Misano
- **Accessibility (4):** Bologna (BLQ) 1 hour, Rimini (RMI) seasonal flights. ✓
- **Atmosphere (4):** Marco Simoncelli tribute adds emotional weight. Rimini Riviera setting helps. ✓
- **VERIFY:** Confirm Rimini (RMI) still has scheduled international flights in 2026.

### Austria (Red Bull Ring)
- **Accessibility (3):** Graz (GRZ) airport + 1-hour drive or Vienna (VIE) + longer. Limited shuttle info. ✓
- **Weather reliability (3):** September at altitude can be cold or beautiful. Variable. ✓
- **Atmosphere (4):** Red Bull Ring typically well-attended. Strong European fanbase. ✓

### Portimao
- **Accessibility (4):** Faro (FAO) 45 min from circuit. Many direct UK routes. ✓
- **Weather reliability (4):** November Algarve is reliable and mild. ✓
- **VERIFY:** "Tickets sold out almost immediately" — confirm 2026 status. 2025 was stated sell-out in Sprint 2.

### Valencia
- **Accessibility (5):** City tram (Line 5) + race shuttle. Valencia airport (VLC) well-served. ✓
- **Atmosphere (5):** Season finale — championship celebrations, riders in paddock. ✓
- **Beginner friendly (5):** Easy tram, clear layout, excellent city base. ✓

### Austin (COTA)
- **Value for money (2):** Highest budget estimate in Sprint 2 ($500+ even at budget tier). ✓
- **Accessibility (3):** Good for North America, poor for Europeans (long-haul). ✓
- **Atmosphere (4):** COTA is a world-class venue; Austin music/food scene adds to experience. ✓
